The Experience

Services

From intimate elopements to grand multi-day celebrations, we design weddings that honour your story while embracing Portugal’s culture, landscapes, and elegance.

Full Planning & Design

From vision to flawless reality.

The complete journey for couples seeking absolute peace of mind. Includes initial style and budget alignment, creative concept development with moodboards, and contract negotiation for all suppliers. We handle the venue scouting with visits to 3 curated locations and manage every logistical detail with total financial transparency.

  • Moodboards & Styling
  • Venue Scouting (3 visits)
  • Financial Transparency
  • Supplier Management
  • Final "Wedding Bible"
  • Onsite Coordination Team

Multi-Day Experience

A celebration that unfolds.

Specialized expertise in managing destination wedding journeys that span several days. From welcome dinners and rehearsal evenings to post-wedding brunches or themed sunset parties, we ensure every guest experience is seamless. We handle all logistics and guest activities, allowing you to focus solely on the celebration.

  • Welcome Dinners
  • Rehearsal Coordination
  • Themed Parties & Brunches
  • Full Logistics Management
  • Guest Activity Planning
  • Bespoke Tour Curation

Bespoke Coordination

Grace under pressure.

For couples who have already planned their details but need expert execution. Includes a final online meeting and rehearsal to ensure every supplier and timeline is strictly followed. On the day, our onsite team manages all logistics and supplier supervision so you can be fully present.

  • 1 Online Briefing Meeting
  • Rehearsal Coordination
  • Onsite Event Team
  • Supplier Supervision

Expert Consulting

Clarity before commitment.

Need a professional second opinion? We offer specialized site inspections—planning visits for tastings, table settings, and decor. Alternatively, our **Online Consult (1.5h)** provides clarity on problem-solving, budget reduction strategies, and timeline guidance for those planning independently.

  • 1.5h Strategic Session
  • Budget Strategy
  • Site Inspection Planning
  • Post-Visit Feedback
